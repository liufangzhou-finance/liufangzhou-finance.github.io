About

I am an Assistant Professor of Finance at the Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. My research lies at the intersection of corporate finance, product markets, and innovation.

Research

I study how information intermediaries and managerial narratives influence firms’ real decisions and long-run value creation, using large language models and textual analysis of earnings call transcripts.
